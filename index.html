It's 2018 bruh. Why the Wii?

You found your way to this site, so I'm guessing you already know the glories of the Wii. Still, it bears stating explicitly the reasons Why the OG Wii is a console worth owning in 2018...

The Wii is cheap.

You can buy a wii at a thriftshop for $20. It will usually come with a couple games or controllers thrown in. You can find a decent CRT TV or early flat panel television that works perfectly with the wii for literally free. These things can be found on craigslist, the dump, or the side of the road.

The Wii makes a handy emulator.

hack.css is a dead simple css framework. Chech out hackcss.com to know more.

The wii is a no-risk way to start hacking consoles.

  1. Make sure you have Jekyll up and running
  2. Clone this repository
  3. Customize it
  4. Push to gh-pages

The wii is a better GameCube than GameCube

  1. Works with gamecube controllers
  2. Better screen resolution
  3. Smash

The Wii will never be easier to get than it is now.

I am not one of these people that views games as an investment vehicle. Furthermore I don't believe that the Wii is in immediate danger of becoming a collector's item any time soon. In fact, I do not understand those people. I suppose they are the same types of folk who enjoy decaffinated coffee, and non-alchoholic beer. I WILL SAY THIS, HOWEVER, I want to own a wii, and they are no longer making them. They will never be MORE common than they are now. The games too, will become increasingly harder to find as time marches on. So, if you want a wii, now is the time to get one.
